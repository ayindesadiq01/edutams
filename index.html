Our Unique Value

EduTAMS provides educational stakeholders with distinctive value, and expandable capabilities

1

Personalization

We offer a completely new user experience that customizes a single solution for different schools in Africa and still enables them to be able to meet their own local peculiarities and needs.

2

Self-Boarding

A Do it Yourself(DIY) approach that offers new experience for administrators to effortlessly stat and complete school set up process in simplified structered stages.

3

Optimised Platform

Lightweight technologies with integrated modules to automate critical schoool procedures, as well as cross-platform data analytics to support decision making.

4

Agency Approach

A third-party agent approach with a personalized platform that provides EduTAMS as a service to schools in exchange for a commission.

Integrated Payment

A fully secured integrated payment gateway that connects bank channels in order toprocess and settle fees/payment collections at exceptionally lower rates.

FAQ

Below, you'll get answers to the questions we get asked the most about our products

What is EduTAMs?

An integrated cloud based school management system that helps school administrators to gain better insight into the management of schools and give teachers, students, and parents the platform to interact on teaching and learning activities.

How secure is EduTAMS School Portal?

The platform is secured and encryptd through: SSL(Secure Socket Layer).

How do I sign up to use EduTAMS School Portal

Go to http://EduTAMS.net/sign-up and register to your school to get started

How secured are my School DAta on EduTAMS School Portal

Your data on the EduTAMS School Portal is safe, secured and also protected by our data protection policy. Everything about your school are stored and stays on your school cloud account on EduTAMS server

What if there is a system crash in our School, what happens to the School Data?

Your data is not stored on your system but in the cloud. So, if your system crashes it does not affect he data stored on EduTAMS

Does Parents, Staff and Student requires Laptops/system to access EduTAMS Portal?

No, EduTAMS Portal is 100% mobile compatible, so you have got no wories, it works jsut fine on mobile devices.

How does EduTAMS Pricing work?

Subscription si termly on the EduTAMS School Portal

Can Parent make payments via EduTAMS Portal?

Yes, Parents can make conveniently make payment and get instant receipt via EduTAMS Portal, using their mobile devices and the payment will reflect in the school account.
